# Forest Ghost 100 — Product Overview

## What It Is
Forest Ghost 100 is a trail challenge management and tracking app built for the Forest Ghost 100 event — a 100-unit (km or mile) outdoor running challenge set in a forest environment. The app centralises participant management, real-time checkpoint logging, and leaderboard visibility so organizers can run the event cleanly and participants can follow their own and others' progress.

## Users & Personas
- **Participants** — runners completing the Forest Ghost 100 challenge who need to log their progress, hit checkpoints, and see where they rank.
- **Event Organizers / Directors** — staff running the event who register participants, manage checkpoints, and maintain field safety awareness.

## Core Features
- **Participant Registration & Profiles** — register runners with bib numbers, names, and categories; full checkpoint history per profile.
- **Checkpoint Logging** — record when each participant passes a checkpoint, with timestamp and notes; fast, accurate, one-tap entry.
- **Live Leaderboard** — real-time rankings by elapsed time, category, or checkpoint reached; auto-refreshes as runners pass through.
- **Progress Tracker** — visual journey through the course: checkpoints hit, distance covered, pace per segment.
- **Event Dashboard** — organizer view: who's on course, who's finished, who's DNF'd — at a glance.

## Positioning
Purpose-built for the Forest Ghost 100 challenge. Not a generic race-management platform — a focused, fast tool for one event done brilliantly.

## Brand & Tone
- **Name**: Forest Ghost 100
- **Tone**: Rugged, focused, serious — the kind of tool trail runners trust
- **Palette**: Forest greens (deep green-700/800), dark slate, amber/orange accent for energy

## Strategic Principles
1. **Speed over complexity** — every action completes in one or two taps.
2. **Real-time clarity** — participants and organizers always know the current state of the event.
3. **Minimal setup** — the event is operational within minutes of opening the app.
